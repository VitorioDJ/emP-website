Where are you at, and what's going on?

Do you feel stuck in a difficult life situation? Do you find yourself at an important crossroad, wondering which direction to take? Are curious to get clarity about who you truly are what you’re really meant to do in this world?

In our personal sessions we help you to see the bigger picture of your life, and to understand the meaning of your current life challenges. We help you to see the gifts hidden in your situation, so that you can understand it, integrate it, and move on in a direction aligned with your values.

In this process, we shine the light on your true gift, and on the obstacles preventing you from seeing and expressing it. With precision and efficiency, we guide you to see and embrace the best parts of yourself, and shed anything less.

How Does It Work?

Vitorio and Evia guide you to see and release old beliefs and trauma that are keeping you stuck in a subconscious loop, so that the new level of life possibilities opens up.

As a result of this process, you will start seeing opportunities that have always been there, but are now visible and available to you.

We work from the space of joy and lightness and believe that even the biggest life challenges can be solved with grace and precision.

The session is approx. 90-min long.
