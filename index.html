Evia and Vitorio are modern day alchemists and visionaries, dedicated to researching the mechanics of consciousness, human experience and nature of reality. Their goal is taking their clients to a lasting state of inspired flow - The Timeline of Inner Bliss.

We take our clients on a journey, from the state of limitation to fully inspired, boundless creativity. Using our custom-developed psychological and alchemical tools, we’re able to reach deep layers of subconscious programming, address the attachments and shift our clients into a different level of reality.

Our approach combines lightness and precision. It is profound, yet doesn't feel heavy. We keep the sense of humor and the ability to laugh at the human comedy we're collectivelly playing.

Our passion is connecting people with their wildest dreams and making these dreams become a reality. We work at high speed - while adjusting to our clients’ ability to integrate change. The more open you are - the faster the results.

Vitorio De Janeiro

Vitorio is a contemporary alchemist and visionary dedicated to exploring inner freedom and applied consciousness. A former entrepreneur, his path has led him to a profound investigation of alchemical and astrological sciences, as well as new technologies. He now develops subtle approaches that connect energy, knowledge, and technology, guiding individuals and organizations toward the expression of their full creative power.

Evia Aur

Evia is a designer and visionary. Her work explores the intersection of art, space, and inner experience. After many years dedicated to creating design concepts inspired by nature, her journey has shifted toward a more subtle exploration of perception, consciousness, and transformation. Through STELL-ART and VIBE-SPACES projects, she creates artistic experiences that invite presence, remembrance, and connection between the visible and the invisible.
